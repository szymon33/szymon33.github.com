Cloud and Multimedia Klett

Klett Group is one of the largest and most respected educational publishers in Europe. They create textbooks for all types of schools since 1898. I have developed couple of simple web services for them.
The first one is called Chmura Klett (in translation 'Cloud Klett') which supports teachers which are using Klett's textbooks. It provides teaching aids for these books. Content includes mostly audio lessons but also video (like experience nature), .PDF files (like tests), pictures and .ZIPs. All is stored on S3. It includes audio streaming technic. Service is public and free but you probably will need Klett's paper book. Take a closer look.
The second app is called Multimedia Klett which is a catalogue with serach engine for all Klett's on-line resources like interactive exercises, e-books, e-shop content, youtube movies, claud (described before) and others spreaded widely thrugh the internet. Take a closer look.

Ecowater

Ecowater is one of the world's largest manufacturers of domestic water treatment systems. They have nearly 90 years of experience in implementing new technology. They provide technical support for installed devices. This is my most advanced CMS so I haven't described any other CMS in this portfolio. The application has three parts. Front end pages which include products, news, promotions, inquiries, articles, interesting facts about water and more. Partners' zone which includes video tutorials for service, simple shop with gadgets, download documents section and more. The last part is CMS in the back-end, to rule them all. As you can see on the first screenshot service has responsive design. Ecowater graphic design is not mine. Many people have worked to write the site's content. Take a closer look with diffrent language versions as well as site locations: Polish, Bulgarian, Czech, Lithuanian and Slovak
