Klett Group is one of the largest and most respected educational publishers in Europe. They create textbooks for all types of schools since 1898. I have developed couple of simple web services for them.

Content includes audio lessons, .PDF files (like tests), pictures and .ZIPs. It is sotred via S3. It includes audio streaming technic. Service is public and free but you probably need Klett's paper book. Take a closer look.
Second called Multimedia Klett which is a catalogue and serach engine for all Klett's on-line materials spreaded widely thrugh internet like: interactive exercises, claud (described before), e-books, movies or e-shop. Take a closer look.

Thule-kolejka

Thule is an international company which offers bike, ski, snowboard, watersport, luggage and multipurpose roof racks. Thule has a major factory in Poland. This simple application is called "Thule – kolejka" (in translation: Thule – queue). It displays a board of the real time counters for awaiting production people. One counter counts down time left for the worker to move on and take the track's documents from the logistic department while another counter counts down time left to move on and just load a truck. Real time counters has been developed via setTimeout() JavaScript function. Based on responsive features application works fine on mobile devices.
