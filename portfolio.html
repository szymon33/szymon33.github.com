Thule-kolejka

Thule is an international company which offers bike, ski, snowboard, watersport, luggage and multipurpose roof racks. Thule has a major factory in Poland. This simple back-end application is called "Thule – kolejka" (in translation: Thule – queue). It displays a board of the real time counters for the production people. One counter counts down time left for the worker to go and take the track's documents from the logistic department or time left to go and load the truck. Real time counters has been developed in JavaScript. Based on responsive features application works fine on mobile devices.

SOS-PPP

SOS-PPP is my first Customer Relationship Management system based on Ruby On Rails. The application was ordered by a law firm called Forex. Forex had the idea to sell legal services, such as preparation of agreements, vindication or help with establishing a company, for a small fee. Their target market became small companies. They soon realized that the number of customers was growing very fast. They also signed an agreement with a similar company which sells different services and merged the product list with them, in cooperation. The company’s Executive Manager Marek Wiśniewski said that SOS-PPP helps the company grow in a balanced way.
For now application consists of the following modules: accounts (including history of account sales), tasks assigned, contacts in relation many to many with accounts, information about payments, telemarketers’ activity monitor, information about products, special document hosting for cooperation purposes, and the administration panel.
